PTE- Person Test of English

The scoring procedure for the PTE Academic test involves several steps and criteria to evaluate a test-taker's proficiency in English language skills.

Scoring Procedure

Here's an overview of the marking procedure for each section of the PTE Academic test:

  1. Writing and Speaking Section

    2. - Automated scoring algorithms assess spoken and written responses based on various linguistic features, including content, pronunciation, fluency, vocabulary, grammar, and spelling.
    - Each task within the Speaking and Writing section is scored separately, and scores are awarded based on the accuracy, relevance, coherence, and completeness of the responses.
    - Scores for speaking and writing tasks range from 0 to 90 points, with increments of 1 point.

  2. Listening Section

    3. - Listening tasks are also scored automatically based on the accuracy and completeness of the responses.
    - Each correct answer earns points, while incorrect or partially correct answers receive no points.
    - Scores for the listening section are reported on a scale of 10 to 90 points, with increments of 1 point.

  3. Reading Section

    4. - Responses to reading tasks are automatically scored based on the correctness and completeness of the answers.
    - Each correct answer earns points, while incorrect or partially correct answers receive no points.
    - Scores for the reading section are reported on a scale of 10 to 90 points, with increments of 1 point.

  4. Overall Score

    5. - The overall score for the PTE Academic test is calculated by summing up the scores obtained in each section.
    - The overall score is reported on a scale of 10 to 90 points, with increments of 1 point.
    - The overall score reflects the test-taker's overall proficiency in English language skills, including speaking, writing, reading, and listening.

  5. Score Report

    6. - Test-takers receive score reports typically within 2-3 business days after the test date.
    - Test-takers receive score reports typically within 2-3 business days after the test date.
    - Score reports are available online and can be accessed through the Pearson website using the test- taker’s login credentials.

  6. Score Validity

    7. - PTE Academic scores are valid for up to two years from the date of the test.
    - Test-takers can use their scores to apply for admission to universities and colleges, as well as for visa and immigration purposes.

Overall, the scoring procedure for the PTE Academic test is designed to provide an accurate and reliable assessment of a test-taker's English language proficiency, based on standardized criteria and automated scoring algorithms.
